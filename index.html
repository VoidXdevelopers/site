Who is Voidx Developers ?

VoidX is run by group of 2 volunteers who are part time android developers. We also share passion for protecting environment, people and wild life. So we came out with an idea of releasing premium paid apps regularly which have no ads, no trackers, no data collection, no privacy violating permissions. The 90% of the revenue generated will be donated to International organisations.

LEARN MORE

Contribution bars

Revenue generated during a month(Day 1- Day 31) is credited to us on 15th of the next month. We contribute to international funds within 24-48 hrs after a small audit.

Contribution approach

1 % equals 10$. As a start, revenue generated during september was 52$. We have contributed those 52$ towards UNICEF. To narrow the organisations, currently we donate the funds to United Nations(U.N) approved organisations. We will update all the earnings, donated amounts regularly in this website. It will include the documents, images with some basic details blurred out (a small gesture towards privacy).

Most of the time we will update the transaction and other important details within the period of Day 16-Day 17 of every month.

WFP

The World Food Programme aims to eradicate hunger and malnutrition. It is the world’s largest humanitarian agency. Every year, the programme feeds almost 80 million people in around 75 countries.

Learn More

UNICEF

UNICEF works in 190 countries and territories to save children’s lives, to defend their rights, and to help them fulfil their potential, from early childhood through adolescence.

Learn More

FAO

The Food and Agriculture Organization leads international efforts to fight hunger. It is both a forum for negotiating agreements between developing/developed countries and undeveloped countries.

Learn More

UNAIDS

The Joint United Nations Programme on HIV/AIDS (UNAIDS) leads and inspires the world to achieve its shared vision of zero new HIV infections, zero discrimination, zero AIDS-related deaths. UNAIDS unites the efforts of 11 UN organizations
